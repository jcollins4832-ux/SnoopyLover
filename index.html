About Snoopy

Snoopy is a beagle and one of the most popular characters from Charles M. Schulz’s comic strip Peanuts. Known for his vivid imagination, the doghouse (his Sopwith Camel), and his friendship with Charlie Brown, Snoopy has become an icon of comics and pop culture.

Fun facts

  • Snoopy first appeared on October 4, 1950, in the Peanuts comic strip.
  • He often imagines himself as the World War I Flying Ace.
  • He lives atop his iconic red doghouse.
